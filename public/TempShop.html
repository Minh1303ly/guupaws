How to Measure Your Pet

Neck

Measure around the base of your pet's neck where a collar would naturally sit.

Chest/Girth

Measure the widest part of your pet's chest, usually just behind the front legs.

Length

Measure from the base of the neck to the base of the tail along your pet's back.

Weight

Weigh your pet to help determine the appropriate size range.

Breed-Specific Sizing Tips

  • French Bulldogs and Pugs typically need wider chest measurements.

  • Dachshunds need longer length but smaller chest measurements.

  • Maine Coon cats often need larger sizes than their weight suggests.

  • Greyhounds and Whippets need narrower chest but longer length measurements.
